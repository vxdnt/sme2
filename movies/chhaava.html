Experience the Grand Historical Saga: Chhaava

A Cinematic Tribute to History

Step into the glorious past with Chhaava, an epic historical drama that brings the legendary tale of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj to life. This cinematic masterpiece promises to be a visual spectacle filled with valor, sacrifice, and an unforgettable narrative that will leave audiences spellbound.

Release Date, Venue & Booking Details

Mark your calendars! Chhaava is set to release in theatres across India on December 22, 2024. This highly anticipated Marathi film will be screened in multiple cinema halls, offering an immersive experience for history buffs and movie lovers alike. Book your tickets now!

A Stellar Cast & Crew

Directed by the acclaimed filmmaker Laxman Utekar, the film stars Vicky Kaushal in the role of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, bringing his legendary persona to life with power and intensity. Backed by a talented ensemble cast, grand production design, and an enthralling musical score, Chhaava promises to be a historical spectacle.

The Legacy & Expectations

The anticipation for Chhaava is sky-high, as audiences eagerly await an authentic portrayal of Maharashtra's proud heritage. The film follows in the footsteps of successful historical dramas and aims to capture the essence of an era filled with courage and sacrifice. Fans expect nothing less than a blockbuster that pays tribute to one of India's greatest warriors.

Impact on Indian Cinema

With its larger-than-life storytelling and a deep-rooted cultural connection, Chhaava is expected to set new benchmarks for historical films in India. It aims to reignite interest in Marathi cinema on a national scale while educating audiences about the bravery and leadership of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj.
